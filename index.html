What's on your plate?

As humans, we are health conscious about the type of food that we consume, so why are we not more conscious about how what we eat affects the health of Mother Earth?

Climate change is a global issue that will affect everyone. Climate change is a complicated problem with a lot of moving parts, and finding a truly effective solution will require significant mitigation, adaptation, legislation, and innovation innovation. In order to tackle this global challenge, policymakers, climate scientists, computer scientists, and journalists will need to work together. We are facing a mammoth of a problem, but we can all do our part and contribute to the solution by being more mindful to something we do everyday: Eating.

Your choices matter!
