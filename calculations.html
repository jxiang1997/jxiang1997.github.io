Calculations

Cheeseburger

Burger patty
$$\frac{\mathbf{5.33} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ burger patty}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{26.45} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of beef}} = \mathbf{4.00} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Cheese
$$\frac{\mathbf{0.6} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ slice of cheese}} \cdot \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \cdot \frac{\mathbf{9.78} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of cheese}} \cdot = \mathbf{0.17} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Lettuce
$$\frac{\mathbf{0.3} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ lettuce leaf}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1.08} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of lettuce}} = \mathbf{0.01} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Tomato
$$\frac{\mathbf{0.7} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ tomato slice}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1.18} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of tomato}} = \mathbf{0.02} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Bun
$$\frac{\mathbf{2.25} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ hamburger bun}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1.18} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of Hamburger buns}} = \mathbf{0.07} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$

Chicken Caesar Salad

Parmesan cheese
$$\frac{\mathbf{0.25} \textbf{ cup}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ parmesan cheese}} \times \frac{\mathbf{8} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ cup}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{9.78} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of parmesan cheese}} = \mathbf{0.55} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Romaine lettuce head
$$\frac{\mathbf{22} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ romaine lettuce head}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1.08} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of romaine lettuce}} = \mathbf{0.67} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Chicken breast
$$\frac{\mathbf{4} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ chicken breast}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{5.05} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of chicken breast}} = \mathbf{0.57} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Focaccia croutons
$$\frac{\mathbf{2} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ slice of focaccia bread}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1.11} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of bread}} = \mathbf{0.06} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Juice of one lemon
$$\frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ ounce}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ lemon}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{0.17} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of lemon}} < \mathbf{0.01} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$

Bowl of Cereal

Milk
$$\frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ ounce}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ bowl of 1% milk}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1.34} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of milk}} = \mathbf{0.04} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Cereal
$$\frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ ounce}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ serving of cereal}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{0.98} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of average cereal}} = \mathbf{0.03} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$

Chips and Guacamole

Avocado
$$\mathbf{5} \textbf{ avocados} \times \frac{\mathbf{0.5} \textbf{ kg}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ avocado}} \times \frac{\mathbf{0.43} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of avocado}}\times \frac{\mathbf{1} } {\mathbf{8} \textbf{ servings}} = \mathbf{0.27} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Red onions
$$\mathbf{\frac{1}{2}} \ \mathbf{ red \ onion} \times \frac{\mathbf{6} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ red onion}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{0.17} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of onion}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} } {\mathbf{8} \textbf{ servings}} < \mathbf{0.01} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Fresh lime juice
$$\frac{\mathbf{1.55} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ lime}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{0.17} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of lime}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} } {\mathbf{8} \textbf{ servings}} < \mathbf{0.01} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Tortilla chips
$$\frac{\mathbf{16} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ bag of tortilla chips}} \times \frac{\mathbf{50} \textbf{ grams} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}} {\mathbf{1.5} \textbf{ ounces of tortilla chios}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{1000} \textbf{grams}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} } {\mathbf{8} \textbf{ servings}} = \mathbf{0.07} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$

Over-easy egg

Egg
$$ \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ dozen eggs}}{\mathbf{12} \textbf{ eggs}} \times \frac{\mathbf{2.7} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ dozen eggs}} = \mathbf{0.23} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$

Slice of Pepperoni Pizza

Mozzarella
$$\frac{\mathbf{8} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ mozzarella cheese}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{9.78} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of cheese}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} } {\mathbf{8} \textbf{ slices}} = \mathbf{0.28} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Pepperoni
$$ ( \frac{\mathbf{14} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ pork}} \times \frac{\mathbf{6.87} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of pork}} + \frac{\mathbf{6} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{ beef}} \times \frac{\mathbf{26.45} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of beef}} ) \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} } {\mathbf{8} \textbf{ slices}} = \mathbf{0.9} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Tomato paste
$$\frac{\mathbf{80} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{} \textbf{ tomatoes}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1.18} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of tomato}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} } {\mathbf{8} \textbf{ slices}} = \mathbf{0.33} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
Pizza dough
$$\frac{\mathbf{27} \textbf{ ounces}}{\mathbf{} \textbf{ flour}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg}}{\mathbf{35.27} \textbf{ ounces}} \times \frac{\mathbf{0.61} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}}{\mathbf{1} \textbf{kg of flour}} \times \frac{\mathbf{1} } {\mathbf{8} \textbf{ slices}} = \mathbf{0.06} \textbf{kg} \ \mathbf{CO_{2}}$$
