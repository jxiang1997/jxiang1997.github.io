Environmental Impact of Various Meats and Dairy

When thinking about the environmental impact of different types of meat, it is important we think beyond just carbon emissions. In addition to green house gas emissions stemming from livestock and the animal agriculture industry as a whole, we need to also consider land use. Livestock covers roughly 45% of the earth's total land, it is highly plausible that more efficient use of land would allow for potential reforestation and implementation of carbon sequestration.
